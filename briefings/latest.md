* **US:** Open-ended ceasefire + blockade = leverage. New sanctions on Iran weapons programs; EU expanding own.

* **Israel:** Netanyahu claims Iran campaigns strengthened Israel's regional position.

**Bottom Line:** The US bought time while the blockade sustains max pressure -- but with Tehran refusing to negotiate under siege and zero proposals on the table, escalation is the probable next move. Watch for: (1) an Iranian conditional proposal within 48 hours of Islamabad talks, validating the truce; or (2) no response, signaling imminent escalation.
